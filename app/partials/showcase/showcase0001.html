REWARDS - A.Amalorpuvamary (III STD CLASS TEACHER)
All of us work. All of us expect result or rewards. But very often we expect more than we deserve. We forget that our reward depends on the work we do.
Kind words spoken to others will bring us kind words from many. Kindness shown and help given will certainly bring us better rewards than we expect. We should do good deeds and speak kind words. Real good is done without any thought of the rewards it will bring. At the same time it is only human expect a good word at least in return. MORAL: He who plants thorns shall not gather roses.
