HOBBIES - A.SATHISH KUMAR( IX STD STUDENT) Hobbies are leisure time activities. Everybody has one interesting hobby or another. Some are interested in writing stories or poems. Stamp collecting is my interesting hobby. During leisure, I collect stamps.As soon as I get a post cover I remove the stamps from it. I have a special album for posting stamps. I paste the stamps on the album. I have collected hundreds of postal stamps. Some of them belong to foreign countries. Some stamps bear the pictures of national animals as birds. A large number of Indian stamps bear the national emblem.Foreign stamps like those of the Gulf countries bear the portraits of their kings. Stamps give us this valuable information about foreign countries as well as our own.
